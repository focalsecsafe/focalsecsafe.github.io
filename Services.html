How to Open

1) Proof of identity – valid passport

2) Proof of address – please provide one of the following and note that all documents must be original and not more than three months old: current household bill – gas, electricity, telephone, water or council tax valid full driving license, current bank statement or credit card statement

3) Two photos

Rental Procedure

1) Applicants must be at least 18 years old

2) Applicants must sign a safe deposit box license and specimen signature card

3) Once approved, successful applicants are required to pay a Key deposit and the annual rental fee for each safe deposit box rented

Access Procedure

Safe deposit box holders (or other authorized parties) are free to access to their boxes at anytime during normal opening hours. Upon verification of the individual's signature (personal identity documentation must be provided by the individual, ), Vault representative will accompany the individual into the vault and unlock the box in his or her presence. We Do not have a copy of secondary key There for no one but you and your nominated party will have access to you valuables.
